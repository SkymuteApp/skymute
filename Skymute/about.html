Effective Date: 19/05/2023

At Skymute, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Skymute mobile application ("App"). By using the Skymute app, you expressly consent to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information:
When you create an account on the Skymute App, we may collect certain personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, profile picture, and any other information you choose to provide. This information is used to create and manage your account, customize your user experience, and facilitate communication between users.

1.2 User-Generated Content:
Skymute collects the content you generate and share on the App, including stories, messages, photos, videos, and any other information you choose to post or send. This content is intended to be shared with other users and to enhance the overall community experience.

1.3 Usage Information:
We automatically collect certain information about your use of the App. This includes device information, such as unique device identifiers, operating system version, mobile network information, and device settings. We also collect log data, including IP address, access times, and pages viewed, as well as app activity data, such as features used, buttons clicked, and settings preferences. This information is used to analyze app usage patterns, improve our services, and provide personalized content and recommendations.

2. Use of Collected Information

2.1 Personalization:
Skymute uses the information we collect to personalize and enhance your experience on the App. This includes suggesting relevant connections, tailoring content, and providing customized features based on your preferences and usage patterns. We may use automated algorithms and machine learning techniques to analyze your data and provide you with a more tailored and enjoyable user experience.

2.2 Communication:
Skymute may use your personal information to communicate with you, including sending important updates, notifications, and promotional materials related to the Skymute App. We may also send you personalized recommendations, content, and offers based on your interests and activity on the App. You can manage your communication preferences within the App settings.

2.3 Improving the App:
Skymute analyzes the usage data we collect to understand how users interact with the App, identify trends, and make enhancements to improve our services. This includes analyzing user behavior, engagement metrics, and user feedback to optimize the App's features, functionality, and overall user experience.

2.4 Legal Compliance:
Skymute may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes. This includes responding to legal requests, enforcing our terms and policies, and protecting the rights, property, and safety of Skymute, its users, and the public.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 User Interactions: As a social networking app, certain information you provide, such as your username and profile picture, may be visible to other users. The content you post, including stories, messages, and interactions with other users, is intended to be shared with the Skymute community. Please exercise caution when sharing personal information or engaging in conversations with other users.

3.2 Service Providers: Skymute may engage third-party service providers to assist us in delivering our services. These providers have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to maintain its confidentiality. They may provide services such as hosting, data storage, analytics, customer support, and marketing.

3.3 Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, sale, or other transfer of all or a portion of our assets, user information may be one of the transferred assets. We will notify you through the App or by email if your personal information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

3.4 Legal Requirements:
Skymute may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as court orders or government regulations. We may also disclose your information to protect our rights, enforce our policies, or investigate potential violations, fraud, or security concerns.

4. Data Security

4.1 Skymute takes reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We implement technical, physical, and administrative safeguards to secure the information we collect and store. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

4.2 It is important that you help protect against unauthorized access to your account and information by choosing a strong password, keeping your login credentials confidential, and logging out of your account when not in use. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

5. Your Choices and Rights

5.1 Account Information: You can review and update your account information, including your profile details, within the App's settings. You can also delete your account and associated data at any time, subject to certain exceptions and legal requirements.

5.2 Communication Preferences: You can choose to receive or opt-out of certain communications from Skymute within the App's settings. You may still receive essential account-related notifications, such as password resets and important updates, even if you choose to opt-out of other communications.

5.3 Location Information: You can control the App's access to your device's location information through your device settings. Please note that disabling location access may limit certain features and functionalities of the App.

5.4 Data Access and Rectification: You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to request corrections or updates to ensure its accuracy. Please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy to make such requests.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

6.1 Skymute may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised policy will be effective when posted. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at [skymute@skymute.com]. We will address any inquiries promptly and to the best of our ability.
